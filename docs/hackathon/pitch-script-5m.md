**[0:40–1:20] Problem**

But there are clear barriers to bringing prediction markets to Japan.

First, Japanese topics are rarely listed on Polymarket or Kalshi. Even when they are, accuracy is low because Japanese residents cannot participate.

Why? Because regulation. In Japan, prediction markets are not yet allowed.

And even if we solve the legal issue, there is the thin market problem. In a local market like Japan, participants and liquidity are both low. In large markets like Polymarket, arbitrage traders correct manipulation. But in a small market, this mechanism does not work.

**[1:20–2:40] Solution**

We designed SENQ by working backwards from these challenges.

**Weighted Betting is** Our core technology. In traditional markets, every user is treated the same. SENQ is different. We weight bets based on user attributes. Even with low liquidity, if the right people are in the market, we keep accuracy high.

**Anonymous Privacy Data** — Users connect their My Number Card or social accounts. My Number Card is a digital ID every Japanese has. We use privacy layers like Midnight to prove attributes without showing personal information. We can also show bet trends by demographics. Not just odds, but who is predicting what.

**Custom AMM** — We start with Parimutuel. As liquidity grows, we move to LSMR AMM, then Orderbook. All custom-built with our weighting system inside.
